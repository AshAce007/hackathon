Rules

  1. 75% of you told us you want to hack all night with your friends and have fun, so we are opening up the hackathon. The submissions need not be Grab only. #GFF
  2. Team Size: 3-4 pax. Please register your teams before 05 September 2016 via this google form.
  3. Hardware entries are welcome. Do note that you have to procure the hardware yourself before the hackathon. The budget for the hardware needs to be pre-approved by your line manager.

Themes

  • BYOI - Bring Your Own Idea: If you already have an idea, you can commence the hackathon if your team is successfully registered.
  • Shark Tank Pitches: If you don't have an idea, don't worry! There will be Shark Tank Pitches for 3 mins by your fellow online team members on 31 August 2016. Pitches will contain business problems that need technological solutions. Here is what a Shark Tank Pitch looks like.
  • Find-Design-Hack: Don't like the pitches either? We'll give you an anonymized dataset and you can "find" the problem you want to solve.
