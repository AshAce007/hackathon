Rules

  1. 75% of you told us you want to hack all night with your colleagues and have fun, so we are opening up the Grabathon. Your Grabathon submissions needn't be solving a problem for Grab; we've opened it up. #GFF
  2. Team Size: 3-5 pax. Please register your teams before 05 September 2016 via this Google sheet.
  3. Hardware entries are welcome. Do note that you have to procure the hardware yourself before the hackathon. The budget for the hardware needs to be pre-approved by your line manager.
  4. Teams are not allowed to use staging for the hackathon. Each team has their own AWS account which they can use to spin up necessary infrastructure.
Don't forget to include your t-shirt size here! For queries and questions please reach out via the official slack channel #grabathon.

Themes

  • BYOI - Bring Your Own Idea

    If you already have an idea, you can commence the hackathon if your team is successfully registered.

  • Shark Tank Pitches

    If you don't have an idea, don't worry! There will be Shark Tank Pitches for 3 mins by your fellow online team members on 31 August 2016. Pitches will contain business problems that need technological solutions. Here is what a Shark Tank Pitch looks like.

  • Find-Design-Hack

    Don't like the pitches either? We'll give you an anonymized dataset and you can "find" the problem you want to solve.
